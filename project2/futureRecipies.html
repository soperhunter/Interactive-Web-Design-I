Oven Oreo Smore's

Source: https://pixabay.com/

Ingredients

  • 12 Oreos
  • 12 Marshmallows
  • 4 Hershey Chocolate Bars

Instructions

  1. Preheat the broiler on your oven
  2. Twist off the tops of your oreos. Line a sheet pan with the cookie bottoms, cream side up. Top each of the cookies with a piece of chocholate, followed by a marshmallow.
  3. Place your S'mores in the oven to broil. Watch them closely as the marshmallow can burn quickly. Broil for about 30 seconds, or until puffed up and golden brown.
  4. Once you've pulled it out of the oven, top each S'moreo with the remaining cookie tops. Enjoy!

Easy Hershey's Chocolate Pie

Source: https://pixabay.com/

Ingredients

  • 6 Hershey Bars
  • 8 oz (1 container) cool whip
  • Ready made pie crust
  • Extra whipped topping for garnish (cool whip)

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate over low heat or in a microwave
  2. Fold melted chocholate into whipped topping until completely blended
  3. Pour/spread mixture into crust and refrigerate until set.
  4. Optionally garnish with whipped topping and shaved chocolate peices.
  5. Chill and serve.

Easy Homemade Caramel Corn

Source: https://pixabay.com

Ingredients

  • 2 Bags Popped Popcorn
  • 1 Cup Butter (2 Sticks)
  • 1 Cup Light Brown Sugar
  • 2 tsp. Vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp. Baking Soda

Instructions

  1. Make Popcorn.
  2. Salt Popcorn to taste.
  3. Melt 1 cup of butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  4. Add 1 cup brown sugar and stir until thoroughly mixed.
  5. Stirring continuously, bring the butter/sugar mixture up to a boil on medium heat
  6. Boil for 4 minutes.
  7. Add vanilla and boil for 1 more minute
  8. Add baking soda and mix
  9. Fold popcorn into caramel sauce
  10. Cool and Enjoy!
