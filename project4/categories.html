Categories


Animals

Everybody loves to look at a good, cute picture of an animal. Any type of animals are allowed from wild to home bodies, but it's important to make sure the images are appropriate for most age.


Food

Food is generally a more difficult category to win on because of the inflation of food pictures in general. However, food is art in itself, and it is encouraged for everyone to try and make dishes or goods that are quality enough to catch the eyes of others.


Nature

Nature is the beauty of the world. Any kind of outdoorsy, or lanscape type photo could be considered nature. We try to be very open with the categories interpretation; while we can't accept every interpretation, we try our best to be open minded as this is a broad category.
