Submission Rules and Guidelines


Submissions

Submission details are explained on the Submissions Page, but here is the general guide. Image format must be either PNG or JPG, nothing else will be accepted. The max file size that will be accepted is 5 Megabytes (MB), anything else will be disqualified. It is very important that the work submitted is verified to be your own, any form of plagarism will result in an immediate ban.


Categories

For details on the allowed categories please visit the Categories Page. Our three main categories are Animals, Food, and Nature. We try to be very flexible with what fits under the topics, but if you have any questions just shoot us an email.


Who Can Enter

Anyone! The submission form will ask what kind of artist you are (Amatuer/Artist/Professional) because we do competitions & feature art for all talented creators.
